# AI Field Impact

**A community-maintained knowledge base tracking how AI is transforming every professional field.**

Every professional is asking: *How is AI changing MY work?* This project answers that question with real data — maintained by practitioners, not influencers.

## What Each Field Covers

| Section | What You'll Find |
|---------|-----------------|
| **AI Tools** | Every AI tool relevant to the field, with adoption levels and pricing |
| **Automation** | What AI can do (fully, partially, assisted) and what it can't |
| **Skills** | Which skills are gaining value and which are declining |
| **Career** | Job market trends, salary impact, new roles emerging |
| **Voices** | Real quotes from practitioners in the field |
| **Updates** | Timestamped news feed of AI developments |
| **Papers** | Research papers and industry reports |

## Contribute

This project is built by practitioners. If you work in a field and know how AI is changing it, you can help.

- **[Add an AI tool](https://github.com/ketanshetye/ai-field-impact/blob/main/docs/CONTRIBUTING.md#add-a-tool)** — 2 minutes
- **[Report outdated info](https://github.com/ketanshetye/ai-field-impact/issues/new?template=outdated-info.yml)** — 1 minute
- **[Write a new field](https://github.com/ketanshetye/ai-field-impact/blob/main/docs/ADDING_A_FIELD.md)** — 1-2 hours

[Full Contributing Guide](https://github.com/ketanshetye/ai-field-impact/blob/main/docs/CONTRIBUTING.md)
