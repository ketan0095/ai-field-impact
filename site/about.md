# About AI Field Impact

## What This Is

AI Field Impact is a community-maintained knowledge base that tracks how artificial intelligence is transforming professional fields across every industry.

For each field, we document:
- Which AI tools practitioners are using
- Which tasks are being automated (fully, partially, or assisted)
- Which skills are becoming more or less valuable
- How careers, salaries, and job markets are shifting
- What still requires human expertise, and why

## Why It Exists

There's no shortage of AI hype. But practitioners need **facts, not predictions** — what tools actually work, what's really changing, and what they should learn next.

This project provides that information, maintained by people who actually work in each field.

## How It Works

- **Anyone can contribute** — add a tool, share a quote, report outdated info
- **Practitioners are prioritized** — people working in a field know it best
- **Everything is sourced** — factual claims require citations
- **Content is verified** — automated freshness checks flag stale data every month
- **It's open source** — CC BY-SA 4.0 licensed, transparent, community-governed
