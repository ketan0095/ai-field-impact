## Overview

The cybersecurity field faces a **4.8 million global talent shortage** while attack volume continues to grow exponentially. AI is the industry's primary response to this imbalance: automating frontline SOC operations so that scarce human expertise can focus on complex investigation and strategic defense.

2026 is the year of the **agentic SOC**. AI agents now autonomously triage alerts, correlate events, and execute response playbooks — a shift confirmed by major launches at RSAC 2026 from CrowdStrike, Check Point, and Saviynt. Gartner predicts 50% of Tier 1 SOC analyst positions will be eliminated or transformed. The direction is clear: **augmentation at scale**, not wholesale replacement.

## ✅ What AI Can Do

<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

#### Fully Automated
*AI does the work, humans review.*

- 📋 **Alert triage & classification** — AI agents classify 90%+ of alerts autonomously
- 📊 **Log analysis & event correlation** — Millions of events/day processed by AI SIEM
- 🦠 **Malware classification** — ML models identify known and polymorphic malware
- 🌐 **Network anomaly detection** — Self-learning AI baselines and flags deviations
- 🎣 **Phishing detection** — AI analyzes email content, URLs, and sender behavior
- 🔍 **Vulnerability scanning** — Continuous automated scanning across infra and code

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

#### Partially Automated
*Humans direct, AI handles subtasks.*

| Task | AI Handles | Human Handles |
|:-----|:-----------|:-------------|
| Incident response | Triage, evidence collection | Root cause, coordination |
| Threat hunting | Pattern surfacing, leads | Hypothesis, investigation |
| Compliance audit | Evidence collection, mapping | Interpretation, decisions |
| Cloud posture mgmt | Config scanning, flagging | Prioritization, remediation |
| Pen testing (routine) | Common attack paths | Novel vectors, chained exploits |

</td>
</tr>
</table>

## ❌ What Still Requires Humans

| Task | Why AI Can't Do This |
|:-----|:---------------------|
| **Security architecture** | Requires business context, risk tolerance, and long-term technology roadmap understanding |
| **Geopolitical threat intelligence** | Nation-state actor assessment requires human judgment about politics and intent |
| **Crisis communication** | Coordinating legal, PR, executives, and regulators requires human diplomacy |
| **Creative ethical hacking** | Novel exploit chains and social engineering require lateral thinking |
| **Insider threat investigation** | Assessing employee behavior in HR/legal context requires human sensitivity |
| **Security awareness training** | Changing organizational culture requires human engagement and connection |
