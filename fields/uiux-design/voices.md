# Voices From The Field — UIUX Design

Real perspectives from practitioners. Submit yours via [issue form](https://github.com/ketanshetye/ai-field-impact/issues/new?template=general.yml) or PR.

---

> "I used to spend 40% of my time on wireframes. Now I spend that time arguing about whether we should build the feature at all. Honestly, it's a better use of my time."
> — Senior Product Designer, Series B startup, 2026-03

---

> "The junior designers we're hiring now need totally different skills. I don't care if you can push pixels in Figma. I care if you can look at an AI-generated screen and tell me what's wrong with it."
> — Design Director, enterprise SaaS, 2026-02

---

> "Synthetic user tools are useful for generating hypotheses but dangerous if you mistake them for real research. We use them to prepare for interviews, not replace them."
> — UX Researcher, health-tech startup, 2026-01

---

> "AI made me a better designer, not a lazier one. I generate 10 layout options in 5 minutes and spend an hour thinking about which one actually solves the user's problem. The thinking is the job now."
> — Mid-level UX Designer, fintech, 2026-03
