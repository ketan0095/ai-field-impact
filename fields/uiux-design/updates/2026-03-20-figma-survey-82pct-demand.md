## What Happened

The UX Design Institute published their "UX Job Market in 2026" report, citing a 2026 Figma survey showing 82% of design leaders said their organisation's need for designers has either increased or stayed the same, with many reporting 10-25% growth in demand. Approximately 70% of hiring managers plan to recruit at least one UX professional.

## What This Means

Despite AI fears, demand for UX professionals remains strong. However, the report emphasizes that roles are evolving — AI is shifting focus toward human skills like empathy, creativity, and strategic judgment. The demand is for different kinds of designers, not fewer designers. Entry-level remains competitive while senior strategic roles grow.
