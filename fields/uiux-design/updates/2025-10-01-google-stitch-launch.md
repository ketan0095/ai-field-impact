## What Happened

Google launched Stitch, a free AI-powered UI design tool through Google Labs. Stitch generates full mobile and web app UIs from text prompts, featuring ideation mode, live editing mode, predictive heatmaps, auto-theming, instant prototypes, and code export. It directly competes with Figma AI and other prompt-to-design tools.

## What This Means

A major tech company entering the AI design tool space with a free tool lowers the barrier to entry for design production. Stitch's inclusion of predictive heatmaps and code export means a single tool can go from prompt to validated prototype to code. This accelerates the commoditization of UI production work and reinforces the shift from "making" to "deciding" for designers.
