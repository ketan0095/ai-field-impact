# AI Field Impact

**How is AI changing YOUR field?**

A community-maintained knowledge base tracking AI's real impact on every professional field — tools being used, tasks being automated, skills gaining and losing value, and what still needs humans.

## Why This Exists

Every professional is asking the same questions:
- **What AI tools should I know about in my field?**
- **Which parts of my job are being automated?**
- **What skills should I learn to stay relevant?**
- **What will always need a human?**

This project answers those questions with **real data, not hype** — maintained by practitioners in each field, not AI influencers.

## What Each Field Covers

Every field directory contains:

| File | What's Inside | How Often It Changes |
|------|--------------|---------------------|
| `overview.md` | Curated summary — AI adoption level, key takeaways | Quarterly |
| `tools.yml` | Every AI tool relevant to this field — structured, sortable | Weekly |
| `automation.md` | What AI can do (fully, partially, assisted) and what it can't | Monthly |
| `skills.md` | Skills gaining value, skills losing value, what to learn | Quarterly |
| `career.md` | Job market trends, salary impact, new roles emerging | Quarterly |
| `voices.md` | Real quotes from practitioners in the field | Anytime |
| `updates/` | Timestamped news — new tools, industry shifts, events | Anytime |
| `papers/` | Research papers and reports with summaries | Anytime |

## How To Contribute

We need **practitioners, not programmers**. If you work in a field and know how AI is changing it, you can contribute.

| What you want to do | Effort | How |
|---------------------|--------|-----|
| Add an AI tool | 2 min | [Edit tools.yml](docs/CONTRIBUTING.md#add-a-tool) or [submit via form](../../issues/new?template=tool-submission.yml) |
| Share a field update | 3 min | [Create an update file](docs/CONTRIBUTING.md#add-an-update) |
| Add a research paper | 3 min | [Create a paper file](docs/CONTRIBUTING.md#add-a-paper) |
| Share a practitioner quote | 1 min | [Add to voices.md](docs/CONTRIBUTING.md#add-a-voice) |
| Report outdated info | 1 min | [Open an issue](../../issues/new?template=outdated-info.yml) |
| Write a new field | 1-2 hrs | [Follow the guide](docs/ADDING_A_FIELD.md) |

Read the full [Contributing Guide](docs/CONTRIBUTING.md) to get started.

## Principles

- **Neutral, not alarmist.** No "AI will replace everyone" fearmongering. Facts and data.
- **Sources required.** Every factual claim needs a citation. No speculation.
- **Practitioner-first.** People working in the field know it best. We prioritize their input.
- **Living document.** Fields are verified for freshness every 6 months. Stale data gets flagged.
- **Open to all.** Non-technical contributions are valued equally. You don't need to code to contribute.
